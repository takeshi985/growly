# Gamification

The first coherent set is deliberately small:

- 10 XP for a completed task; harder tasks may later award up to 15 XP.
- Daily goal: three completed tasks.
- Streak: a gentle count of active learning days, with no threatening copy.
- Stars: one to three per lesson based on completion and support used.
- Badges: deterministic milestones tied to genuine learning.

XP feeds level and daily-goal progress. Stars communicate lesson quality.
Badges mark durable achievements. Chests and collectible items are deferred
until tested with families. No mechanic removes progress or blocks learning.
